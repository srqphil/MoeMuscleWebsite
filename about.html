About

Moe's Mission

To build strong passionate people! To make permanent lifestyle changes, to help you realize you have always been capable of living the lifestyle you desire. We are bringing back the personal in personal training. We are here to make you stronger, more knowledgeable about your body and mind, more confident, more balanced, more energetic, and most importantly healthier from the inside out.

Moe's Biography

EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND: Bachelor Degree in Science from Kent State University. Physical Education teaching license Catholic University of Brasilia.

CERTIFICATIONS: American Council on Exercise/ACE Certified Personal Trainer, Clinical Exercise Specialist.

Moe’s areas of interest include weight management, functional training, sports conditioning and special populations (older adults, cardiovascular and orthopedic conditions, diabetes, osteoporosis, etc). He is a strong advocate for self-health and wellness. Rom believes that there must be a balance between exercise and nutrition in wellness programs in order to combat health risks. He believes that creating a healthier body takes a commitment to making lifestyle adjustments. Moe’s goal is to help clients make that adjustment successfully.
